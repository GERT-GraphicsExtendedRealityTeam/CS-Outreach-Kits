CS Outreach Kits for Teachers & Students

Inspire, Aspire, Accomplish

 

Be Brunel School outreach kits are for teachers and students (pupils Years 8 - 12). The materials presented below enable learning about coding and programming through making, engaging, challenging, and collaborating. These kits help promote CS areas in a hands-on, practical way and are well-suited for in-class activities.

As the CS Athena SWAN outreach team at Brunel, we ran a number of different activities for schools and colleges to raise awareness of computing and its applications. Our main goal is to inspire more women and girls to pursue computer science. Our activities range from talks at open days to hands-on workshops on campus and/or at local schools. Some resources related to those hands-on workshops can be found below.
